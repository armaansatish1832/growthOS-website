Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · GrowthOS, India

What GrowthOS is: GrowthOS is an AI-powered seller intelligence platform that connects to your selling platforms, reads your performance data, identifies revenue leaks, and delivers actionable fixes. We are a read-only analytics tool. We never write to, post on, or modify anything on your selling platforms.

Data we access

When you connect a platform, GrowthOS requests read-only access to your performance metrics — impressions, orders, revenue, ROAS, and similar analytics data. We access only what is necessary to generate your weekly report. We never access your customers' personal information, payment details, or any data beyond what your platform's analytics dashboard shows you.

What we never do

  • We never sell your data to any third party
  • We never store your platform passwords — all connections use secure OAuth tokens
  • We never share your business metrics with competitors or other sellers
  • We never make any changes to your platform listings, ads, or settings
  • We never access your customer data, order addresses, or payment information

How we use your data

Your platform data is used exclusively to generate your GrowthOS reports and recommendations. We use aggregated, anonymised patterns across the platform to improve our benchmarking models — but your individual business data is never shared or identifiable in this process.

Data security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Platform connections use industry-standard OAuth 2.0. You can disconnect any platform and request deletion of your data at any time by emailing armaan@getgrowthosapp.com.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any data we hold about you. You can disconnect GrowthOS from any platform at any time from within your platform's connected apps settings. To request full data deletion, email armaan@getgrowthosapp.com and we will process your request within 7 business days.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions: armaan@getgrowthosapp.com
